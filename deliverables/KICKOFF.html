03 — Brand Direction

Three directions.
One decision.

Each concept speaks to a different buyer persona and projects a distinct type of authority. The choice determines every visual and copy decision for the next 30 days.

CONCEPT 01
The Surgical Elite
Archetype: The Specialist
"Extracting Elite LatAm Engineering Signal."
Dark, precise, and technically authoritative. Designed to resonate with highly technical CTOs who view hiring as an extraction of pure engineering signal from market noise. Mirrors the environments they work in — dark-mode IDEs, terminal interfaces, data dashboards.
Dark / Cyan / White
Primary buyer CTOs and VPs of Engineering at high-growth SaaS companies
CONCEPT 02
The Enterprise Safe-Bet
Archetype: The Institutional Partner
"Risk-Free Enterprise Nearshore Scaling."
Bright, structured, and compliance-forward. Engineered to eliminate the anxieties of CFOs, procurement officers, and legal counsel. Projects regulatory maturity, total risk elimination, and long-term stability. The visual language of a trusted institutional firm.
Navy / Platinum / Blue
Primary buyer CFOs, procurement officers, and legal counsel at enterprise firms
CONCEPT 03
The Agile Integrator
Archetype: The Catalyst
"Seamless Nearshore Pod Integration."
Clean, modern, and energetically forward-looking. Focuses on the fluid integration of LatAm engineering pods into U.S. technical teams. Appeals to Directors of Engineering and Operations who need to scale without disrupting existing agile rituals. Modern SaaS aesthetics.
White / Graphite / Emerald
Primary buyer Directors of Engineering and Operations at scaling tech companies
